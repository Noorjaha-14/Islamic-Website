Basic Principles

🌙 5 Arkaan-e-Islam (Five Pillars of Islam)

1️⃣ Shahadah (Iman ka Aitiraaf)

“La ilaha illallah Muhammadur Rasulullah.”
👉 Yani: Allah ke siwa koi ilaah nahi, aur Muhammad ﷺ Allah ke Rasool hain.

📖 Qur’an se Daleel:
* “Allah gawah hai ke uske siwa koi ma’bood nahi...” (Surah Aal-e-Imran 3:18)
* “Aur humne aap se pehle koi Rasool nahi bheja...” (Surah Al-Anbiya 21:25)
📜 Hadith:
Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jo shaks dil se Shahadat de... Allah usay Jahanum se bacha lega." (Bukhari, Muslim)

✨ Ahmiyat: Shahadah Islam ka pehla aur sabse buniyadi rukun hai.
✅ Status: Mandatory aur Islam ki entry gate.

2️⃣ Salah (Namaz)

👉 Din mein 5 waqt ki Namaz farz hai.

📖 Qur’an se Daleel:
* “Namaz qayam karo, zakat do...” (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:43)
* “Beshak Namaz fahashi aur burai se rokti hai.” (Surah Al-Ankabut 29:45)
📜 Hadith:
Nabi ﷺ ne farmaya: "Sabse pehle jis cheez ka hisaab hoga woh Namaz hai." (Tirmidhi)
"Banday aur kufr/shirk ke darmiyan farq Namaz hai." (Muslim)

✨ Ahmiyat: Namaz roohani ghiza hai aur Allah se direct connection hai.
✅ Status: Mandatory (Farz) — 5 waqt ka.

3️⃣ Zakat (Maal ki Saaf-safai aur Ghareebon ka Haq)

👉 Apne maal ka ek hissa (2.5%) ghareebon, yatimon, aur mohtaajon ko dena.

📖 Qur’an se Daleel:
* “Aur Namaz qayam karo, zakat do...” (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:110)
* “Zakat to sirf fuqara, masakeen...” (Surah At-Tawbah 9:60)
📜 Hadith:
Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jis ko Allah maal deta hai aur woh zakat nahi deta..." (Bukhari)
"Zakat maal ko kam nahi karti." (Muslim)

✨ Ahmiyat: Zakat maal ko paak karti hai aur society mein insaf ka nizaam banati hai.
✅ Status: Mandatory (agar nisab ho).

4️⃣ Sawm (Roza)

👉 Ramzan ke mahine mein subah se shaam tak khana, peena aur shahwatiyat se rukna.

📖 Qur’an se Daleel:
* “Ae Imaan walon! Tum par roza farz kiya gaya...” (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:183)
* “Ramzan ka mahina jis mein Qur’an nazil hua...” (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:185)
📜 Hadith:
Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jo shaks Ramzan ke rozay imaan ke saath rakhe..." (Bukhari, Muslim)
"Rozedaar ke mooh ki boo Allah ke nazdeek misk se bhi zyada pyari hai." (Bukhari)

✨ Ahmiyat: Roza taqwa paida karta hai, sabr aur self-control sikhata hai.
✅ Status: Mandatory (sirf Ramzan ke dinon mein).

5️⃣ Hajj (Allah ke Ghar ka Safar)

👉 Zindagi mein ek martaba Hajj farz hai jo qudrat aur istita’at rakhta ho.

📖 Qur’an se Daleel:
* “Aur Allah ke liye logon par Hajj farz hai us ghar ka, jo qudrat rakhta ho safar ki...” (Surah Aal-e-Imran 3:97)
📜 Hadith:
Rasulullah ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jo Hajj kare aur usme faasik aur badkirdgi se bache, woh paak ho kar laut ta hai jaise naya paida hua." (Bukhari, Muslim)
"Umrah se doosri Umrah tak ke darmiyan ke gunah maaf hote hain, aur maqbool Hajj ka sawaab jannat hai." (Bukhari, Muslim)

✨ Ahmiyat: Hajj insaan ko duniya se kaat kar sirf Allah ke saamne khada karta hai. Sab Musalman barabar ho jaate hain — rang, mazhab, khandan sab khatam, sirf Tawheed aur Ibadat bachi rehti hai.
✅ Status: Mandatory (agar qudrat ho).

🌟 Nateeja

Ye paanch Arkaan Islam ke buniyadi pillars hain.
👉 Shahadah – Aqeedah
👉 Salah – Ibadat ka nizam
👉 Zakat – Maashiyat ki paaki
👉 Sawm – Nafas par control
👉 Hajj – Ummat ki wahdat aur Allah ki ibadat

Jis tarah bina sthambhon ke imaarat khadi nahi hoti, waise hi in paanch ke baghair Islam mukammal nahi hota.
